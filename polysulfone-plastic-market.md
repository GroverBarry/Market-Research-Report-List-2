It is widely employed in various industries, including automotive, electrical, and healthcare, due to its ability to withstand high temperatures and harsh environments. PES (Polyethersulfone) is a similar type of thermoplastic with exceptional dimensional stability and resistance to chemicals. PPSU (Polyphenylsulfone) is another variation with enhanced impact strength and transparency. All three types of polysulfone plastics find applications in demanding environments where durability and performance are crucial.</p></p>
