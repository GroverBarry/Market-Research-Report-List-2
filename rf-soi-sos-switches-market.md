RF SOI switches are based on a silicon substrate with an insulating layer, providing high isolation and low insertion loss. On the other hand, RF SOS switches utilize a sapphire substrate, which offers excellent RF performance and high power handling capabilities. Both types of switches are critical in applications such as telecommunications, wireless communication, and radar systems, where efficient RF signal routing and switching is required.</p></p>
