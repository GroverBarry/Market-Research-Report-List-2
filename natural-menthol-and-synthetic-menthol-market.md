<p><p>Natural menthol is a compound derived from the leaves of peppermint, spearmint, and other mint plants. Natural menthol has a cooling and soothing effect and is commonly used in products like toothpaste, cough drops, chewing gum, and topical analgesics.</p><p>On the other hand, synthetic menthol is a chemically produced compound that mimics the properties of natural menthol. It is manufactured through a process of catalytic hydrogenation of thymol or by combining natural menthol with other chemicals. Synthetic menthol is considered to have similar characteristics and applications as natural menthol, but it is usually cheaper in price.</p><p>According to market analysis, the natural menthol and synthetic menthol market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.6% during the forecast period.
