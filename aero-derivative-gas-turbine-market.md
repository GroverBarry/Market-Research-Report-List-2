<p><p>Aero Derivative Gas Turbine Market consists of two main types: Closed Cycle Technology Gas Turbine and Open Cycle Technology Gas Turbine. Closed Cycle Technology involves a gas turbine integrated with a heat recovery system, where exhaust heat is captured and used to produce additional power, increasing overall efficiency. Open Cycle Technology, on the other hand, operates by taking in air, compressing it, adding fuel for combustion, and expanding the hot air through a turbine to produce power. Both technologies are utilized in the aero derivative gas turbine market for various applications such as power generation and aviation.</p></p>
