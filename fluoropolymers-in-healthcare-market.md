PTFE is known for its non-stick properties and is used in medical devices, surgical implants, and as a coating for medical equipment. PVDF is highly resistant to chemical and thermal degradation, making it suitable for catheters, connectors, and filtration devices. PVF finds applications in fluid transfer systems and IV sets, whereas FEP is used as a barrier film. PCTFE is used in barrier applications like pharmaceutical packaging.</p></p>
