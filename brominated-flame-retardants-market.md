<p><p>Brominated flame retardants (BFRs) are a group of chemicals that are used to improve fire safety by slowing down the spread of flames in various products, including electronics, furniture, textiles, and building materials. However, BFRs have been under scrutiny due to their potential adverse effects on human health and the environment.</p><p>The future outlook of the brominated flame retardants market is expected to be influenced by several factors. Firstly, there is growing concern over the toxicity and persistence of BFRs, which has led to increased regulations and restrictions on their use.
